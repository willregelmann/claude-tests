Add retry with exponential backoff to the upload client

Transient 503s from the storage backend were surfacing as hard failures.
The client now retries idempotent uploads up to 3 times with backoff
(250ms, 500ms, 1s) and surfaces the final error if all attempts fail.

- Wraps PutObject in retryWithBackoff
- Adds RETRY_MAX and RETRY_BASE_MS env knobs (defaults 3 / 250)
- Covered by upload_retry_test.go

Closes #482
